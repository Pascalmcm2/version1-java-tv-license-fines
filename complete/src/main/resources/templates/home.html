Pay a TV Licence fine


This service is used to pay a TV Licence fine. To pay a TV Licence fee click here

You can pay a TV Licence fine online or by phone.

How to pay

You will need your credit or debit card to pay for the fine.

You must pay the fine by the date given in your notice of fine. The court might reject your payment if enforcement action has already started


Online Payment

You will need to login using your Government Gateway User ID & Password.



Other ways to pay
Call 0300 790 9901 to pay a fine in England
Call 0300 790 9980 to pay a fine in Wales
